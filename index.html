    • What happens if bot fails to understand what my users want?

      ORI is designed to handle 100% conversations using a plethora of intelligence built into it. ORI bots learn continuously and rapidly to avoid upsets. In the off-chance that the bot cannot respond to the customer, it hands over the chat to an agent to salvage the customer experience at that point. This hybrid approach has humans as a backup when required.
